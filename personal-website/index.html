About Me

What do you think of when you hear the word leader? Perhaps you think of a famous person in history, or perhaps someone that has left a positive impression on your life.

A leader to me is a person that can has accepted the responsibity of being transparent and vunerable while at the same time guiding others to success. A leader has made mistakes but has not failed.

I have had the opportunity to do a lot of things in life that I have enjoyed, yet never was where I was supposed to be. Many people would say that I am a lost soul. As a matter of fact, they have.

Nah, I am a leader.

So, lets get personal...born Jessica Amanda I quickly learned that no matter how easy your name is-people will always try and change it. So, I decided around adolenence that 'Jess' and 'Jessie' were not my favorite names for me. It was baffling that 'Jessica' was just too much say. Lord knows, I wouldn't want to trouble anyone by reciting a three syllable name.

I had to think what was easy but it didn't sound embarrasing at the same time. Then it happened! While switching classes in 10th grade; I heard an upper classman basketball player yell from the other side of the large rotunda yelling 'J'. Well, he actually said "hey I know you...you are...something with a J, right?" I decided to forget the fact he did not really know me outside of the boy I called myself dating at the time.

There it was, a new creation and new identity, easy, chic, and unforgettable. Long story short. My name is Jessica Amanda. I go by Jai

I am a southern girl with upstate vibe. I enjoy being creative and writing because it fuels my soul and empowers others. I have worked in the corporate world, I have taught, I have counseled etc. I laugh at my jokes even when no one else does. Don't really care either. I believe that a life filled with variety is a fulfilling one. I really enjoy chocolate ice cream and pasta. Not together, but definately on the same day!

Lastly, but most imprtantly, my heart grooves to four perfect verses over a tight beat. My family.

Okay, enough about me and all the sappy stuff. Go look around the site!

Enjoy!

Jai
